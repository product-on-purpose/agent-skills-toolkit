01Executive summary

A well-built skill that does its job and triggers reliably; the one gap is an instruction budget that should be trimmed, not a defect.

acme-write-blog-post drafts a long-form post from a brief, outline-first, with an SEO pass. It is structurally conformant to the agentskills.io skill rules and the Standard's skill clauses, with a single advisory warning: the body runs to about 240 instructions, over the ~200 guidance, which risks later steps being dropped. Behaviorally it is solid: across a 20-case triggering set it fired correctly 19 times, with one near-miss (an ambiguous "help me write" prompt). The qualitative review rates output quality strong: clear structure, good voice control, honest SEO suggestions.

Recommended next move: move the SEO checklist into a references/ file loaded on demand (closing the budget warning), and add one trigger keyword to catch the ambiguous-prompt near-miss. Both are quick and raise reliability without changing what the skill does.
